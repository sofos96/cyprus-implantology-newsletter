"Advancing Dentistry, Transforming Lives"

Welcome to Cyprus's Premier Implantology Program

The Cyprus Academy of Implantology stands as the first and most comprehensive implantology education program in Cyprus, designed specifically for dentists seeking to master the art and science of dental implantology. Our academy represents a groundbreaking initiative in postgraduate dental education, offering an unparalleled learning experience that combines theoretical excellence with hands-on practical training.

Program Highlights

378 Hours of Training

Comprehensive theoretical and practical program covering all aspects of oral implantology

English Language

All courses conducted in English to ensure international accessibility and standards

Expert Faculty

Learn from renowned specialists in prosthodontics, oral surgery, and implantology

Hands-On Experience

Practical training with real cases and state-of-the-art equipment
